Nidetect

Discover Etsy Marketplace Trends

Nidetect is a research tool for Etsy sellers. It analyzes publicly available marketplace data via Etsy’s official API to identify trending product niches, especially for small and new shops. Our goal is to help independent creators discover opportunities and grow their businesses.

With Nidetect, sellers can spot high-demand, low-competition niches and make smarter decisions about their products and marketing.
